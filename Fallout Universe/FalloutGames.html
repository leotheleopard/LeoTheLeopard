The Fallout Games In A Nutshell


Official Games:


Fallout (1997)

A Post Nuclear Role Playing Game!

Fallout follows the story of The Vault Dweller, The Vault's water chip has broken and they have been sent to get a new one. For the Vaullt.




Fallout 2 (1998)

A Classic RPG (Minus the Faeries, Spells, and other Crap)

Fallout 2 is the story of The Chosen One, a descendant of The Vault Dweller, on a journey to fnd a GECK to save their tribe from starvation.




Fallout 3 (2008)

Prepare For The Future

The Lone Wanderer travels the wastes of post-war Washington, D.C. in search of their father after he leaves the Vault they called home for 16 years.



Fallout: New Vegas (2010)

Enjoy Your Stay!

After getting a shott to the head, a Courier for the Mojave Express sets out to find the one who shot them and to finish the delivery, deciding the fate of the Mojave in the process




Fallout 4 (2015)

Welcome Home!

After waking up from a 110 year cryosleep, the Sole Survivor of Vault 111's expierminet runs throuugh the wastes of Boston to find their son and to avenge his partner.




Fallout 76 (2018)

Our Future Begins

Walk back into the world of Fallout as a Dweller of Vault 76, reclaiming Appalachia only 35 years after the Great War. The people have all gone, it's upu to you to rebuild huumanity here.


Semi- or Non-Canon Games:


Fallout Tactics: Brotherhood Of Steel (2001)

Submit to the Brotherhood. Fight with the Brotherhood. Conquer for the Brotherhood.

A Brotherhood Of Steel Initiate is sent with other initiates on a journey across Kansas to eliminate multiple threats to the wastes, resulting in a battle that will change the Midwest wastes for years to come.




Fallout Shelter (2015)

A Better Future, Underground

In preperation of Fallout 4, Bethesda released a mobile game! Fallout Shelter has you play as the Overseer of a Vault, build rooms, go on quests, explore the wastes. This game is non-canonical and contradicts information by Canonical games.




Fallout: Brotherhood OF Steel (2004)

Welcome To The Wasteland

In preperation of Fallout 4, Bethesda released a mobile game! Fallout Shelter has you play as the Overseer of a Vault, build rooms, go on quests, explore the wastes. This game is non-canonical and contradicts information by Canonical games.


Upcoming And Rumoured Games


Fallout Official Pen And Paper (2020)

An expansion to Fallout: Wasteland Warfare

Explore the wastes with your friends in a standalone tabletop RPG expansion for Fallout: Wasteland Warfare. Developed by Modiphius Entertainment using their patented 2d20 System.




Possible Fallout 3 Remaster (2020)

We Dunno About This Yet

In early February 2019, a Fallout styled video was uploaded onto the Bethesda Amazon page 3 times, labelled as Xbox One, PS4, and PC. Some people speculate a remaster of an older game.
